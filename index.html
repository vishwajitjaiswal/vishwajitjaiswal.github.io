Vishwajit Jaiswal

PhD Scholar, Indian Institute of Technology Bombay

Hello everyone, I am Vishwajit R. Jaiswal, currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Water Resources Engineering at IIT Bombay. My research focuses on understanding human-water feedbacks in the command areas of large reservoirs and developing a socio-hydrologic model to bridge the gap between water demand and supply.

Traditional reservoir models often overlook the human dimension, which can limit their applicability and effectiveness. A socio-hydrologic model integrates stakeholder feedback into conventional reservoir models, enabling a more holistic and realistic approach to addressing the demand-supply challenges. This integration is the primary objective of my research, aiming to provide more sustainable and efficient water management solutions.
